About Us

Crafting Visual Stories with Heart

Welcome to Lathish Photography - Mangalore's premier destination for wedding, pre-wedding, portrait and event photography. Based in the heart of Mangalore, we have spent over 25 years capturing the most precious moments of life with artistic precision and genuine emotion.

Our philosophy is simple: every photograph should tell a story. From the quiet glance between newlyweds to the joyful chaos of a family celebration, we are there to freeze those fleeting seconds into timeless art. We serve clients across Mangalore, Udupi, Manipal, Kasaragod and all of Coastal Karnataka.

What We Offer

Our Services

Wedding Photography

Full-day wedding coverage capturing every ritual, emotion, and candid moment. From the mehendi ceremony to the final farewell, we document your love story with cinematic artistry.

Pre-Wedding Shoots

Romantic couple sessions at stunning locations across coastal Karnataka. We craft intimate, editorial-style portraits that celebrate your unique bond before the big day.

Event Photography

Corporate events, birthday celebrations, engagements, and cultural functions — we bring the same dedication and artistry to every event, big or small.

Baby Shoot

Precious newborn and baby milestone sessions in a safe, comfortable studio environment. Capturing those tiny fingers, sleepy smiles, and first moments of wonder.

Maternity Shoot

Celebrating the beauty of motherhood with elegant, glowing maternity portraits. Indoor studio sessions and outdoor golden-hour shoots tailored to your comfort.

Cinematic Videography

Cinematic wedding films and highlight reels that you will treasure forever. Drone footage, slow-motion sequences, and professional color grading for a movie-like experience.
